Welcome to TeamAlerts!

TeamAlerts is a dynamic online platform designed to foster seamless communication and collaboration between students and teachers.
As a user, you can join TeamAlerts either as a student or a teacher.

Teachers can easily create rooms to organize their classes, make important announcements, and schedule events.
Students can apply to join these rooms and stay updated with all the latest announcements and schedules.

TeamAlerts ensures an interactive and efficient educational experience for everyone involved.
Join us and enhance your teaching and learning journey today!
